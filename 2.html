Blue Cube

Work Name: Blue cube

Author's Name: Hu Yueyi

Materials: Arduino, Screen

Statement:

This art installation is my exploration of the fusion between interactivity and aesthetics, a piece known as the "Blue Cube." Created using TouchDesigner, I envisioned an interactive experience with the audience, seamlessly blending music, dynamics, and innovation. The "Blue Cube" is not merely a static artwork; it is a dynamic, vibrant entity that expands and contracts organically in sync with the rhythm of the music. The intertwining of visual and auditory elements immerses the audience, creating a distinctive sensory experience.

Through Arduino control, I endowed this blue cube with the ability to rotate. This interactivity serves not only to pique the curiosity of the audience but also to inject dynamism and change into what would otherwise be a static artwork. By manipulating this art installation, viewers become direct participants, fostering a connection between the creator and the audience. The choice of unique materials is another key element in my design, imparting a distinct texture and appearance to the "Blue Cube." I selected these materials not only for their aesthetic qualities but also for their responsiveness and performance in interactive settings.

In engaging with the "Blue Cube," I hope the audience can sense the harmony and dialogue between different elements, sparking contemplation on art, technology, and interactive experiences. This work represents my unique exploration of creative expression and technological innovation, inviting a collective creation of beauty with the audience.
